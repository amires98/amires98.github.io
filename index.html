I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Chicago. My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF), preference alignment, and bandits. I am broadly interested in the mathematical foundations of machine learning, with applications to computer vision and natural language processing.

Prior to UIC, I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering at K. N. Toosi University of Technology, Tehran, where my thesis focused on windshield reflection removal. I also interned at the Technical University of Munich in Summer 2021, working on motion planning for autonomous vehicles using RL, supervised by Prof. Matthias Althoff.
